James Conrad wins the 2021 PDGA Worlds Championship!

In spectacular fashion, James Conrad throws in a 200ft field ace on the 18th hole for the birdie, forcing a sudden death playoff with 5 time world champ Paul Mcbeth. Conrad secures the win on the first of three holes in the sudden death round, parking his drive only 10 feet from the basket while Paul Mcbeths drive went out-of-bounds.
